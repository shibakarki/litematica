Meet Shiba Karki

Hello! I'm Shiba Karki, the creator behind CrafTech. I started this channel to share my passion for gaming, tutorials, and livestreams with the world. My journey began with a simple love for video games, and I decided to create content to help others explore this exciting universe.

My Journey

From the moment I picked up a controller, I knew I wanted to share my gaming experiences with others. Whether it's providing tips, sharing gameplay strategies, or just having fun in livestreams, my goal is to create a welcoming community for gamers of all ages.

When I first started my YouTube channel, I never imagined the incredible journey that lay ahead. What began as a hobby—sharing my love for Minecraft and creating tutorials—soon became something much bigger than I had anticipated.

My journey started on December 25, 2022, when I reached my first milestone: 10 subscribers. On the same day, my videos reached 1,000 views, proving that my content was beginning to resonate with viewers.

By February 2023, the channel had grown to 100 subscribers and 20,000 views. This early success gave me the motivation to push harder, improve my content, and create videos that people would truly enjoy.

When we hit 500 subscribers in June 2023 and 100,000 views in July, I knew we were onto something special. September 2023 brought another huge moment: my first 1,000 subscribers. Now, with over 1,800 subscribers and 400,000+ views, the journey feels more rewarding than ever.

Looking back, I see how far I’ve come, and looking forward, I can’t wait to see where this journey takes me next. The journey is far from over, and I’m excited for what lies ahead!
